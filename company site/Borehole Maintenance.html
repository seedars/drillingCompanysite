SERVICE: MAINTENANCE & CONTINUITY PLAN

PREVENT DOWNTIME

Many of Seedars Clients that rely on their water supply borehole to keep their processes and production plants running.

An unforeseen failure of the borehole could disrupt business and cause major financial losses if plans were not in place for actions on what to do in such an event.

offers peace of mind to Clients in the form of a Business Continuity Plan to ensure minimal downtime in the event of a catastrophic failure of their borehole.

The plan features all actions needed to be carried out to prevent any excessive down time and can be used for planned maintenance of the borehole during periods of factory shutdown.
