Our Service

Seedars Boreholes provide drilling in Rocky formation and in coastal areas as well. We are providing Geophysical Survey, Boreholes Drilling, Borehole Rehabilitation, Mining, Core Drilling, Hand Pumps Supply and Installations, Submersible Pumps Supply and Installation service all over Nigeria.

Geophysical Survey

Boreholes start from the initial step like surveying the site to completing the work. We use modern equipment to locate aquifer line and drilling depth. The company ensures supply of machine spares and tools as per the requirement and the work will be completed in a quick time.

Water Drillng Service

We provide drilling in Rocky Formation and in Coastal areas as well. The drilling operations will be taken up with combination Rigs mounted on 6x4 trucks with ELGI 1100/300 model compressors to a depth of 300 meters and beyond or by hand Augur, Calyx, Rotary and DTH Methods.
